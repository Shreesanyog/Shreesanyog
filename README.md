From building scalable full-stack web applications and orchestrating cloud-deployed AI agents to designing event-driven neuromorphic chips and low-level electronic circuits, I love turning complex problems into efficient, high-performance solutions.

Building technology is not just a skill for me — it's a way of life. Whether I am deploying a CI/CD pipeline on Azure, integrating a Gemini LLM into a React dashboard, or simulating Verilog networks on an FPGA, I bring my all to every project across the technology stack.

### Collaboration and Growth 🌱
